INTRODUCTION

My name is Linqing Hu, known as Luna by my friends. I am currently applying for PhD to pursue my dream of becoming a biologist/Bioinformatician. This is to develop technologies and discover the unknown as my ultimate career goal!

Looking at my education timeline, I completed the full high school curriculum in China and the two years of A Level study on Math, Chemistry, Biology, and Design and Technology in the UK.

After that, I completed my undergraduate education in Biotechnology at The University of Edinburgh and was supervised by Prof Sander Granneman on a project to study the cleavage site of endoribonuclease RNase III in MRSA to discover potential new drug targets.

Then, I completed my Master's degree on Bioinformatics and Systems Biology at the University of Manchester, supervised by Prof Simon Hubbard on a project of protein structure study, and Prof Gisela Orozco on a project of ATAC-seq, RNA-seq, and GWAS combined study to decipher the mechanism of autoimmune diseases. Please find more details in the sections below.

I am actively looking for a PhD opportunity starting in Fall 2025!
